## What Makes Tens City Different?

Instead of complex databases and authentication systems, Tens City takes a different approach:

- **Markdown-first**: Write your posts in simple, portable markdown files
- **Content-addressable**: Every post is uniquely identified by its content
- **Static by nature**: Fast, secure, and simple to deploy
- **Schema.org ready**: Automatic structured data generation for SEO

## The Tent City Philosophy

Think of it as a tent city rather than skyscrapers:

- Anyone can stake a corner ‚Äî no permission required
- Minimal structure that belongs to you
- No complex governance ‚Äî just simple, honest shelter for your ideas
- Your content lives in files you control

## Get Started

Writing a post is as simple as creating a markdown file with frontmatter:

```markdown
---
title: My First Post
description: An example blog post
datePublished: 2025-11-03T00:00:00Z
author:
  name: Your Name
  type: Person
tags:
  - example
  - blog
---

# My First Post

Your content goes here!
```

That's it. No accounts to create, no databases to configure. Just files, served directly to readers.
